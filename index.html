MyCellar

Allows you to manage the wines in your wine cellar. You can enter the wines you have or have had previously and then display what you have currently in your cellar.

This is a simple application to test out Bootstrap, KnockOut and Node.js interactions. The site is hosted currently on http://balontz.atwebpages.com.
